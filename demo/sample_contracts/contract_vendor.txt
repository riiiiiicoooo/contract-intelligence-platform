VENDOR SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Vendor Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into effective June 1, 2023 ("Effective Date"),
between Acme Corporation, a Delaware corporation ("Client"), and CloudTech Solutions Inc., a California
corporation ("Vendor").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Vendor provides IT infrastructure services including cloud hosting, security monitoring, and
technical support;

WHEREAS, Client desires to procure such services under this Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein:

SECTION 1: SERVICES
Vendor shall provide the following services:
- Cloud hosting infrastructure (AWS, Azure, or equivalent)
- 24/7 monitoring and alerting
- Security patch management
- Technical support (4-hour response time for critical issues, 24-hour for standard)
- Monthly capacity planning and optimization reports

TERM AND RENEWAL

SECTION 2: TERM AND RENEWAL
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall have an initial term of three (3) years
("Initial Term"). This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms ("Renewal Terms")
unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least one hundred twenty (120) days prior to
the then-current expiration date. In the event either party fails to provide such notice, this Agreement
shall automatically renew on the same terms for an additional one-year period.

SECTION 3: TERMINATION FOR CAUSE
Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach upon thirty (30) days written notice to
the other party, provided that the breaching party is given an opportunity to cure such material breach
within the thirty (30) day notice period. If the breach is not cured within thirty (30) days, the Agreement
shall terminate at the end of the notice period.

SECTION 4: TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE
Client may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon one hundred twenty (120) days written notice to
Vendor. Upon such termination, Client shall pay all accrued fees through the termination date plus a
termination fee equal to three (3) months of the then-current annual fees.

SECTION 5: FEES AND PRICE ESCALATION
5.1 Base Fees. The annual fees for services shall be as follows:
- Year 1: $500,000
- Year 2: $515,000
- Year 3: $530,450

5.2 Price Escalation. Commencing in the fourth year of this Agreement, Vendor fees shall automatically
increase by three percent (3%) annually on each anniversary date of this Agreement. Client may object to
such annual increases by providing written notice within thirty (30) days of the invoice for the new year.
If Client objects, the parties shall negotiate in good faith regarding the increased fees.

5.3 Invoice and Payment. Vendor shall invoice Client monthly in advance for the services. All invoices
are due and payable within thirty (30) days of invoice date. Vendor may suspend services if payment is
more than fifteen (15) days overdue.

SECTION 6: CHANGE OF CONTROL
6.1 Notice Requirement. If Client undergoes a Change of Control (as defined below), Client must notify
Vendor in writing within thirty (30) days of such Change of Control event.

6.2 Vendor Termination Right. Upon receipt of notice of Client's Change of Control, Vendor may terminate
this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice. In the event Vendor exercises this termination right,
the final invoice shall include termination fees equal to three (3) months of the then-current annual fees.

6.3 Definition. "Change of Control" means: (a) any transaction in which a person or group acquires
beneficial ownership of more than 50% of the voting securities of Client; (b) any merger or consolidation
involving Client unless the entity continues to own at least 50% of the voting power; (c) any sale of
substantially all assets of Client.

SECTION 7: ASSIGNMENT
Client may not assign this Agreement without Vendor's prior written consent, which may be withheld in
Vendor's sole discretion. Notwithstanding the foregoing, Client may assign this Agreement to an acquirer
in connection with a Change of Control, subject to Vendor's termination right under Section 6.2.

SECTION 8: LIMITATIONS ON LIABILITY
Neither party's aggregate liability arising under or related to this Agreement shall exceed the total
fees paid by Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. This limitation does not apply to:
(a) indemnification obligations under Section 9;
(b) breach of confidentiality obligations;
(c) either party's gross negligence or willful misconduct.

SECTION 9: INDEMNIFICATION
Vendor shall indemnify Client from any third-party claims that the services or infrastructure provided
by Vendor infringe any intellectual property rights, provided Client notifies Vendor of such claim in
writing and grants Vendor sole control of the defense and settlement.

SECTION 10: SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT
10.1 Uptime Guarantee. Vendor shall maintain at least 99.5% system uptime, measured monthly. Uptime
is defined as the percentage of time that Vendor's services are available and functioning as intended.

10.2 Service Credits. If Vendor fails to meet the uptime guarantee:
- 98.5% - 99.4% uptime: 5% monthly fee credit
- 98.0% - 98.4% uptime: 10% monthly fee credit
- Below 98.0% uptime: 25% monthly fee credit

Service credits are Client's sole remedy for service failures.

SECTION 11: CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party agrees to maintain confidentiality of the other's proprietary information disclosed under this
Agreement. Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination for three (3) years.

SECTION 12: DATA PROTECTION
Vendor shall comply with all applicable data protection laws, including GDPR, CCPA, and similar
regulations. Vendor shall maintain reasonable security measures to protect Client's data and shall notify
Client within 24 hours of any suspected data breach.

SECTION 13: GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California, without regard to conflicts of
law principles. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration administered by JAMS in
San Francisco, California.

SECTION 14: ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the services
provided hereunder and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties execute this Agreement as of the Effective Date.
